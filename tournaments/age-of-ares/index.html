Tournament Info

Defenders: Age of Ares is a two-stage tournament consisting of a 72 team group stage followed by a 32 team single elimination. The tournament will take place over multiple weekends and feature many favorite maps from House of Cores, Conquest, and Guardians of the Wool, as well as some new additions. Team sizes are 12-20 players with 7-10 players on the map at time. Check out the information thread for more details.
